I'M ON TARGET WITH DOCTOR WHO (Target Boos publishing) - a promotional button badge (1980's)

RETRO STU posted a photo:

A button pin badge issued by the TARGET BOOKS publishing company to promote their Doctor Who book series. Target Books specialised in books that novelised the Dr. Who TV series and were established in 1973 They ceased business in 1989 and in 1990 Virgin Books bought Target's rights and continued to publish the Dr. Who books until 1994.
