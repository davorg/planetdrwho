Daleks are a fictional extraterrestrial race of extremely xenophobic mutants created for the BBC by Terry Nation and designed by Raymond Cusik. They are the mortal enemies of Time Lords, particularly Dr. Who. They appeared in both the original and revived TV productions of Dr. Who and in both motion pictures. The creature inside the mechanical casing is a repulsive and vicious octopus type creature, normally bright green.

The Sudbury Dalek is one of the original Daleks (number 4) used by the BBC and was purchased for the collection as part of the Exploring Childhood project funded by the National Lottery. The project was designed to represent childhood images from the second half of the twentieth century. Restoration work included stabilising the upper section and repair to the plunger
