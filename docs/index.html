A sequence of 4 pictures. The Special Weapons Dalek from the 1988 Dr Who story; "Remembrance of the Daleks"
This is a Resin and metal kit, 8ins (20cm) tall.
It was a very lovely kit to build and the painting was really enjoyable, with the use of Gold, Satin White, Gun metal (All Humbrol Enamels) and the the really fun part; the staining and weathering of this really stained and grubby Dalek casing.
I intend to make a scenic base/backdrop for it in the near future.
